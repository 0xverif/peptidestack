What are peptides? (30 second version)

Peptides are short chains of amino acids - basically small proteins that signal your body to do specific things. They're not steroids. They work with your body's natural processes.

Different peptides do different things:

  • GHK-Cu - Signals your body to produce more collagen (skin, healing)
  • BPC-157 - Accelerates tissue repair and healing
  • CJC-1295/Ipamorelin - Tells your body to release more growth hormone naturally
  • GLP-1s (Semaglutide, etc.) - Regulate appetite and metabolism for fat loss

They're injected subcutaneously (under the skin, like insulin). The protocol tells you exactly how to do this safely.
